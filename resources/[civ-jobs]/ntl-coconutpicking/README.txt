HOW TO INSTALL:

- Drag & Drop file in your resources folder.
- ensure ntl-coconutpicking in your server.cfg

Add item in your shared/items.lua:

['coconut'] 			 = {['name'] = 'coconut', 				['label'] = 'Coconut', 		['weight'] = 1000, 		['type'] = 'item', 		['image'] = 'coconut.png', 				['unique'] = false, 	['useable'] = false, 	['shouldClose'] = false,	   ['combinable'] = nil,   ['description'] = ''},


=================================

DEPENDENCIES:

- qb-core
- qb-target
- ps-ui (https://github.com/Project-Sloth/ps-ui) (Optional)
